Rajma Chawal

Red kidney beans, or rajma in Hindi, are cooked in a hot, sour stew that is flavored with a variety of spices and aromatics to create the well-known North Indian cuisine known as rajma chawal. Rajma and rice are a hearty and reassuring combo that are frequently offered during family gatherings.

Ingredients:

  1. Onions X2
  2. Tomatos X2
  3. Beans X1 cup
  4. Rice X1 cup
